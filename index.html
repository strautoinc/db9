Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to transport a vehicle?

Most deliveries are completed within 3–7 business days, depending on the distance and route availability.

Do I need to be present for pickup or delivery?

Yes, either you or a designated person must be present to sign off at pickup and delivery locations.

What types of vehicles do you transport?

We handle cars, SUVs, trucks, motorcycles, and even specialty vehicles—open or enclosed transport.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Yes. All our partner carriers are fully insured and compliant with federal DOT regulations.
