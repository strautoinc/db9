Frequently Asked Questions

How much does car transport cost?

Costs vary depending on the distance, vehicle type, and transport method. Get a free quote today.

Is my car insured during shipping?

Yes, all vehicles are fully insured while in transit.

How long does shipping take?

Typically between 1 to 10 days, depending on the pickup and delivery locations.

Can I ship personal items in my car?

We advise against it. Personal items are not insured and may delay transport.

What's the difference between open and enclosed transport?

Open transport is cheaper and common. Enclosed transport offers full protection for luxury or classic cars.
