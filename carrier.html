Why Partner with DB9?

At DB9 Logistics, we value our carrier partners. We understand the challenges of the road and aim to make your experience as smooth and profitable as possible. Join our network and gain access to consistent freight, fair rates, and reliable payments.

Benefits of Partnering:

  • Access to a wide network of shippers
  • Competitive rates for your services
  • Quick and reliable payment processing
  • Dedicated support team
  • Opportunities for long-term partnerships

How to Get Started:

Becoming a carrier partner with DB9 Logistics is simple. Just follow these steps:

  1. Contact us through the form below or give us a call.
  2. Provide us with your company information and required documents (insurance, operating authority, etc.).
  3. Once approved, you'll gain access to our available loads.
  4. Choose the loads that fit your routes and schedule.
  5. Deliver the freight and get paid!
