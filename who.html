Who We Are...?


Your Trusted Vehicle Transportation Partner!

DB9 Logistics is a specialized vehicle transportation company dedicated to providing safe, efficient, and professional auto transport solutions across the country. We work with individuals and businesses of all sizes to coordinate car shipping for any purpose—whether you're relocating, managing fleet logistics, or looking for a reliable partner to move vehicles at scale. Our strength lies in reliability, transparency, and partnerships built on trust.

Individual Vehicle Transport Services


We provide secure, nationwide car transport services for individuals in all kinds of situations. Whether you're moving, starting college, relocating for military service, or buying a vehicle out of state, we ensure your car gets where it needs to go—on time and in perfect condition.


  • Door-to-Door Transport
    We pick up your vehicle from your specified location and deliver it directly to your destination, providing maximum convenience and peace of mind.
  • Open Transport
    Our most economical option, open transport is ideal for standard vehicles. Your car will be securely loaded onto an open carrier and transported with care.
  • Enclosed Transport
    For high-value, classic, or luxury vehicles, we offer enclosed transport to provide full protection from the elements and road debris during transit.
  • Classic and Exotic Vehicle Transport
    We specialize in transporting classic and exotic vehicles with the utmost care and attention to detail, ensuring they arrive in pristine condition.
  • Military Vehicle Transport
    We understand the unique needs of military personnel and offer specialized services for transporting vehicles during relocations or deployments.
  • College Student Vehicle Transport
    Moving to college? We provide affordable and reliable transport options for students needing to ship their vehicles to campus.
  • Auction Vehicle Transport
    We work with auto auctions to provide fast, reliable transport for vehicles sold at auction, ensuring they reach their new owners quickly and safely.
  • Fleet Vehicle Transport
    For businesses with multiple vehicles, we offer fleet transport solutions that are efficient, cost-effective, and tailored to your needs.
  • Relocation Vehicle Transport
    Moving across the country? We provide comprehensive vehicle transport services to make your relocation as smooth as possible.
  • State-to-State Vehicle Transport
    We offer reliable state-to-state vehicle transport services, ensuring your vehicle arrives safely and on time, no matter the distance.
  • Seasonal Vehicle Transport
    Whether you're moving your car for the winter or summer, we provide seasonal transport solutions to fit your schedule and needs.
  • Cross-country Vehicle Transport
    Our cross-country transport services ensure your vehicle is handled with care and arrives at its destination safely, no matter the distance.
  • Local Vehicle Transport
    We offer local vehicle transport services for short-distance moves, ensuring your vehicle is delivered safely and efficiently within your city or region.
