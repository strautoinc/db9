Safety First

We do all the work to maintain the safest environment for our clients, partners, employees and the public, so you can focus on what matters most.

We partner exclusively with trusted carriers who share our commitment to safety.

Our Commitment to Safety

At DB9 Logistics, safety is our top priority. We understand that transporting vehicles involves risks, and we take every precaution to ensure the safety of your vehicle and our team.

We adhere to strict safety protocols, including regular vehicle inspections, driver training, and compliance with all transportation regulations.

Our carriers are fully insured and licensed, providing you with peace of mind throughout the transport process.

Safety Steps We Take

  • Thorough background checks for all carriers
  • Regular vehicle maintenance and inspections
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage for all transports
  • Real-time tracking of your vehicle during transport
  • 24/7 customer support for any safety concerns

We believe that transparency is key to safety. That's why we provide you with access to all transport documentation, including inspection reports and insurance certificates.

Carrier Safety & Compliance Standards

At DB9 Logistics, we only work with FMCSA-registered and fully insured carriers who meet our strict safety criteria. We regularly audit our carrier network to ensure:

  • Valid DOT and MC numbers
  • Active insurance on file (cargo + liability)
  • Consistent on-time delivery records
  • No major safety violations on file
  • Experienced and verified drivers

We also require all carriers to complete a safety training program before they can accept loads through our platform.

Technology & Real-Time Monitoring

We use advanced technology to track every vehicle during transport and reduce risks on the road:

  • GPS-enabled vehicle tracking
  • Driver & carrier compliance checks before dispatch
  • Real-time delivery status updates
  • Digital logs and inspection reports
  • Secure client access to documentation

Our platform allows you to monitor your vehicle's journey in real-time, providing transparency and peace of mind.

Safety Tips for Vehicle Owners

  • Remove all personal items from your vehicle before transport.
  • Document the condition of your vehicle with photos before pickup.
  • Ensure your vehicle is in good working condition (e.g., no leaks, sufficient fuel).
  • Communicate any special instructions to your carrier.
  • Keep a copy of your transport agreement and contact information handy.

By following these tips, you can help ensure a smooth and safe transport experience.
